\#\# Visual system  
  
\*\*Palette\*\* — warm paper canvas + near-black ink, with \*\*four accents that cycle\*\* (never one  
flat accent):  
  
| Token | Hex | Role |  
|---|---|---|  
| \`--paper\` | \`\#faf9f9\` | canvas (never pure white) |  
| \`--ink\` | \`\#181718\` | text, ink buttons, inverted panels (never pure black) |  
| \`--blue\` | \`\#2562e7\` | primary accent, \`em\` highlight, links, focus ring |  
| \`--mint\` | \`\#01dcb4\` | accent 2, "alive" dot |  
| \`--pink\` | \`\#fa1e88\` | accent 3, error state |  
| \`--yellow\` | \`\#fae902\` | accent 4 (use ink text on yellow for contrast) |  
  
The accent cycle \*\*blue → mint → pink → yellow → blue\*\* is a brand fingerprint: chip dots,  
proof bars, the rail gradient, and the scroll-graded glow all rotate through it. For a single  
accent default to \*\*blue\*\*.  
  
\*\*Type\*\*  
\- Display / headlines: \*\*Bricolage Grotesque\*\* (\`opsz 12..96\`, weight 600–800), tight tracking  
 (\`-0.03em\` to \`-0.04em\`), leading \`0.93–1.02\`.  
\- Body / UI: \*\*Inter\*\* (400–900).  
\- Idiom: \`em\` is \*\*not italic\*\* — it recolors a word to \`--blue\`. Hero accent words use a  
 blue→pink→mint clipped gradient.  
  
\*\*Other tokens\*\*  
\- Icons: \*\*Phosphor\*\* via Iconify (\`ph:\*\`) — see the \`iconify\` skill.  
\- Texture: a subtle \*\*SVG fractal-noise film grain\*\* at \`opacity .05\`, \`mix-blend-mode: multiply\`.  
\- Glass: frosted cards — \`rgba(250,249,249,.82)\` + \`backdrop-filter: blur(14px)\`, hairline  
 \`\#1817181a\` border.  
\- Radius scale: cards \`18px\` / large \`26px\` / pills & buttons \`999px\` (deliberate, not uniform).  
\- Easing: expo-out \`cubic-bezier(.16,1,.3,1)\`. Always honor \`prefers-reduced-motion\`.  
\- Container max-width \`1240px\`.
