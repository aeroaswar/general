\# MMI Due Diligence Checklist

\#\# Purpose

Create diligence checklists for nickel trading, mining, smelter, logistics, financing, or partnership deals.

\#\# When to use

Use before commitment, signing, advance payment, shipment, or board approval.

\#\# Output format

\#\#\# Diligence Scope

\#\#\# Document Request List

\- Company deed and latest amendments

\- NIB and tax registration

\- IUP/IUPK and related approvals

\- RKAB approval if relevant

\- MODI/MOMS/ESDM verification

\- Surveyor/lab reports

\- Production or stockpile evidence

\- Sales/purchase contract draft

\- Tax/royalty payment evidence

\- Bank account confirmation

\- Beneficial ownership disclosure

\- Litigation/default disclosure

\#\#\# Site/Operational Checks

\- Stockpile location and access

\- Hauling route

\- Jetty/port capacity

\- Barge/vessel availability

\- Weighbridge process

\- Sampling process

\- Moisture control

\- Security and chain of custody
