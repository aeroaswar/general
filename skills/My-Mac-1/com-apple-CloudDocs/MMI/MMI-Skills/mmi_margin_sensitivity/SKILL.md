\# MMI Margin Sensitivity

\#\# Purpose

Build pricing, margin, and cash-cycle sensitivity for nickel trades and mining-related transactions.

\#\# When to use

Use when comparing deal economics, price formulas, shipment options, freight costs, ore specs, or payment terms.

\#\# Required inputs

\- Commodity type

\- Tonnage

\- Ni grade

\- Moisture

\- Price formula

\- Base price

\- Premium/discount

\- Freight

\- Barging/trucking

\- Surveyor/lab cost

\- Royalty/tax if applicable

\- Working capital cost

\- Payment timing

\- FX rate
