\# MMI Document Review

\#\# Purpose

Review contracts, term sheets, invoices, POs, LOIs, MoUs, surveyor reports, RKAB/IUP files, and shipment documents.

\#\# When to use

Use when a document is uploaded or pasted for commercial/legal/risk review.

\#\# Output format

\#\#\# Executive View

\- Overall risk level: High / Medium / Low

\- Recommendation: sign / revise / pause / reject

\#\#\# Red Flags

\- Ambiguous pricing formula

\- Weak payment protection

\- Missing quality adjustment

\- Unclear title transfer

\- No delivery default remedy

\- No surveyor/lab mechanism

\- Unbalanced penalties

\- Weak governing law/dispute clause

\- Regulatory/documentation gap
