Research FAQ

Handling, logistics and compliance — the essentials before you order.

01 How should peptides be stored?
Lyophilised (freeze-dried) peptides are stable at −20 °C and tolerate short transit at ambient temperature. Once reconstituted, store at 2–8 °C and use within the window noted on the CoA. Protect from light and repeated freeze–thaw cycles.
02 How are compounds reconstituted?
For in-vitro work, reconstitute with bacteriostatic or sterile water added slowly down the vial wall — do not shake. Swirl gently until fully dissolved. Reconstitution volume depends on your protocol's target concentration; our team can advise on request.
03 What purity standard do you guarantee?
Every lot is independently analysed by HPLC and mass spectrometry to confirm identity and a minimum of 98% purity (most lots exceed 99%). A Certificate of Analysis accompanies each lot.
04 How does shipping & cold-chain work?
Orders are dispatched in protective, temperature-managed packaging with tamper-evident seals. Domestic delivery within Indonesia typically arrives in 2–4 working days; international timelines are quoted per destination.
05 What payment methods are accepted?
Bank transfer and major e-wallets in IDR. After you send your compound list we return an itemised quote; payment is settled before dispatch. Prices are indicative and confirmed at quotation.
06 Are these products for human use?
No. All compounds are sold strictly for in-vitro laboratory and research use. They are not drugs, supplements, food or cosmetics, and are not intended for human or veterinary consumption, diagnostic, or therapeutic use. By ordering you confirm you are a qualified researcher or institution.
