# disertasi-threshold

**Status:** active · **Area:** [[Academia, Law & Public Sector]]

## What it is
My **doctoral dissertation proposal** (presented as an interactive web infographic):
**"Rekonstruksi Ambang Batas Parlemen Pasca-Putusan MK 116/PUU-XXI/2023"** —
reconstructing Indonesia's parliamentary threshold to be constitutional & proportional.

## Thesis
- Lock a **formula, not a fixed percentage**; simplify via **district magnitude (dapil)**,
  not the threshold. *"Ambang batas yang dihitung, bukan yang ditawar."*
- Core concepts: **Effective Threshold**, **Maximal Threshold**, **Dynamic Threshold**.
- Method: normative / doctrinal legal research (statute + conceptual + case approach).
- Structure: 5 chapters; 12-month work plan.

## Three research questions
- Translate the MK mandate into an objective calculation; reduce wasted votes while
  keeping party simplification; design a constitutional + proportional threshold.
