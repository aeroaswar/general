# ANI — Adhita Nikel Indonesia

**Status:** active · **Area:** [[Ventures & Business]]

## What it is
PT Adhita Nikel Indonesia — holder of the **IUP-OP nickel mine** (Halmahera Timur)
that supplies ore to [[MMI — PT Mangkuluhur Mineral Indonesia]]. PT Bumi Nusa Permai
(BNP) is majority shareholder. Runs the pit-to-barge grade-control SOP (MMI docs/10).
