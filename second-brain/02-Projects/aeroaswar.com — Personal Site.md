**Status:** active · **Area:** [[Jetski Racing & Athletic Identity]]

## 🎯 Outcome
A cinematic athlete site that tells the pure-jetski story (hero → origin → palmarès
→ velocity → gallery → contact), in two moods.

  frosted-glass sheets, cinematic camera glide-in (no countdown), palmarès category filter,
  real race-photo parallax gallery. `window.__aero` snap + `?cap=` capture mode.
