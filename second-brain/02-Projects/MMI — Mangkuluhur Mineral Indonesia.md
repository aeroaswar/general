## What it is
Indonesian **nickel-ore trading & logistics** company (PT, founded 26 May 2025,
Jakarta). Buys **FOB** at the mine jetty (Halmahera Timur, North Maluku) and sells
**CIF** to Sulawesi smelters — earning the trade margin. Does **not** hold a mining
permit; gains the right to lift/sell ore via consortium with PT Bumi Nusa Permai (BNP).
