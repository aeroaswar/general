# IJBA Master Plan 2026–2030 (full notes)

> Source: "IJBA Master Plan 2026–2030" PDF (CONFIDENTIAL — internal). Parent: [[IJBA — Jetski Federation]].

**IJBA = Indonesia Jetsport Boating Association** — the single KONI-affiliated body
governing & growing jet ski sport across Indonesia's 17,000 islands. Est. 2026.
**#RideTheArchipelago** · ijba.id · IJSBA international partner · 34 provincial chapters.

## Headline targets (by 2030)
- **75,000 members**, **50 JetHub** locations, **500+ licensed racers**, **30K JetPass** subs.
- **IDR 131.5B** annual revenue (Y1 IDR 8.5B); **IDR 3B** prize pool/season.
- **AJGP** international series across **8 nations**; **zero-fatality** safety record.

## Six strategic pillars
1. **Organization** — federation under Indonesian law, full KONI affiliation; DPP board, Exec Committee, Technical Commission, 34 chapters.
2. **National Series** — IJBA Nusantara Cup, 8 rounds Mar–Nov.
3. **International** — Asian Jetsport Grand Prix (AJGP), Indonesia founding 40% stake.
4. **Public Access** — JetHubs, Rider Academy, JetPass, Community Race Days.
5. **Revenue** — memberships, JetHub network, JetPass, sponsorship, media rights, academy.
6. **Safety** — ISO-aligned, GPS tracking, speed limiters, rescue teams; zero fatalities.

## Governance
Ketua Umum (Chair/DPP) · 2 Wakil Ketua (Sport Racing & Athlete Dev; Commercial & Growth) ·
Sekretaris Jenderal · Bendahara · Direktur Teknis · Komisi Atlet · Komisaris Independen.

## Nusantara Cup (national championship)
- **7 classes:** Cadet Cup (≤130HP, 14–17) · Sport GP (≤180HP, 16+) · **Pro Runabout** (≤250HP, premier) ·
  Pro Ski stand-up · Women's Open (≤250HP) · Freestyle · E-Jet (electric, 2028).
- **8 rounds:** Jakarta (Ancol) → Bali (Tanjung Benoa) → Manado (Bunaken) → Batam → Makassar (Losari) →
  Labuan Bajo (Komodo) → Banjarmasin (Barito) → **Mandalika Grand Finale** (Lombok).
- Prizes: IDR 50M race win · IDR 250M championship · IDR 75M Rookie.

## Public access programs
- **JetHubs** (50 by 2030): IDR 150K/20min, ~21 machines/hub, 40 km/h beginner limit, ~IDR 218M/hub/mo.
- **Rider Academy** (4 levels): Splash → Cruise → Thrill → Race Ready (IDR 350K–2.5M).
- **JetPass** (30K subs): Lite/Standard/Unlimited/Family (IDR 500K–3.5M/mo); rev target IDR 468B by 2030.
- **Community Race Days** (120/yr): entry IDR 250K, spectator IDR 50K, ~IDR 48M/event.
- Pathway: JetHub walk-in → Community Race Day → Amateur → Nusantara Cup → AJGP.

## AJGP (international)
Founding 2027 (ID 40% stake, TH, MY) → +PH/VN/SG 2028 → +AU/JP 2029 → 11 nations 2030
(IJSBA world-title bid). USD 10K/race winner, 25–30 grid, 3-day weekends. Broadcast: Fox Sports Asia (2028).

## 5-year roadmap
- **2026 Foundation** — legal, board, brand, 2 JetHubs, 4-round Cup → 5K members, IDR 8.5B
- **2027 Growth** — KONI bid, 10 JetHubs, AJGP 4-nation → 25K, IDR 25B
- **2028 Scale** — KONI recognition, 25 JetHubs, AJGP 6-nation, E-Jet → 40K, IDR 55B
- **2029 Excellence** — 50 JetHubs, IJSBA events → 60K, IDR 95B
- **2030 Legacy** — 500+ racers, 11-nation AJGP, world-champ bid → 75K, IDR 131.5B

## Membership tiers
Explorer (free) · Basic Rider (IDR 500K/yr) · Sport Competitor (IDR 2M) · Pro (IDR 5M) ·
Team/Club (IDR 10M) · Corporate Partner (custom).

## Safety framework (ISO-aligned)
Mandatory gear (vest + kill-switch, SNELL/ECE helmets, CE-L2 vest) · live GPS + 10 m proximity
warnings · medical response <90 s (2 rescue skis, paramedics, AED) · machine homologation (engine
sealing, 95 dB, fuel sampling) · GPS speed management tied to Academy level · environmental
(4-stroke by Y2, 2% revenue carbon offset, coral no-ride zones, E-Jet 2028).
