**description:** Load full context for Glu (shop-glu.com), Aero's D2C premium minimalist
innerwear brand. Use whenever the conversation involves Glu, intimate apparel, Shopify ops,
Indonesia D2C, the planned Singapore expansion, sub-brand architecture (Glu Sleep / Men /
Move / Maternity), retail sequencing, content strategy, or the consumer-brand side.

## Entity snapshot
- **Business:** D2C premium minimalist innerwear / intimate apparel · **Market:** Indonesia (primary), Singapore (planned) · **Platform:** Shopify
- **Brand architecture (planned):** Glu Sleep, Glu Men, Glu Move, Glu Maternity
- **State:** full brand audit + homepage redesign + two-volume 2026–2030 playbook (growth, brand architecture, content, retail, community, Singapore runbook)

## Brand identity
- **Aesthetic:** premium minimalism, quiet luxury, negative space, earned restraint.
- **Voice:** confident without proving it; calm, direct; never cute/loud; noun-led copy.
- **NOT:** fast fashion, micro-influencer-driven, cheap, "fun", price-led, novelty.

## Operating context
- Margin-sensitive Indonesia D2C (landed cost, shipping, returns, COGS).
- Channels: Shopify D2C primary; IG / TikTok Shop ancillary; offline pop-up/retail later.
- Customers: 25–40 urban, design-conscious, brand-aware, quality-led.
- Singapore expansion runbook exists (legal, logistics, press, creator seeding, pop-up) — reference, don't recreate.

## Working assumptions / guardrails
- Premium-anchored pricing; **discount language is brand-toxic**. AOV via bundle architecture, not BOGO.
- Photography = highest-leverage asset. Community precedes paid acquisition. Retail = brand amplification, not revenue.
- Don't add a sub-line/category without subtracting; don't borrow fast-fashion moves; **protect the quiet**.
- Indonesian for customer-facing; English/bilingual internal.

**Strategic posture:** the asset is the brand (the minimalism), not the product. Most growth
requests are additions; the right answer is often subtraction.
