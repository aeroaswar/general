## mmi-deal-screen
**Purpose:** analyze nickel trading / mining / smelter / logistics / financing opportunities for PT Mangkuluhur Mineral Indonesia.
**When:** reviewing any proposed deal, shipment, offtake, mining allocation, smelter supply, or partnership.
**Output:**
- **Call:** Strong Pass / Small Option Bet / Core Position
- **Deal Snapshot:** counterparty · commodity (ore / saprolite / limonite / NPI / ferronickel / matte / MHP) · tonnage · grade/spec · price formula · payment terms · delivery terms · expected margin · cash cycle
- **Upside:** commercial · strategic · optionality
- **Strategic Fit:** trading book · smelter/miner network · capital allocation
- **Key Risks** (severity + mitigation): margin · payment · spec/quality · logistics · regulatory · counterparty
- **Diligence:** licenses (RKAB/IUP/IUPK) · capacity/stockpile · surveyor/lab · payment bankability · route/freight · contract enforceability
- **Next move:** proceed / renegotiate / pause / reject + specific action

## mmi-due-diligence-checklist
**Purpose:** diligence checklists for nickel deals. **When:** before commitment, signing, advance payment, shipment, or board approval.
- **Scope:** legal · regulatory · commercial · financial · technical · operational · ESG/reputation
- **Doc requests:** deed/amendments · NIB & tax reg · IUP/IUPK · RKAB · MODI/MOMS/ESDM · surveyor/lab · production/stockpile · contract draft · tax/royalty evidence · bank confirmation · beneficial ownership · litigation/default disclosure
- **Site/ops checks:** stockpile location/access · hauling route · jetty/port capacity · barge/vessel availability · weighbridge · sampling · moisture control · chain of custody
- **Go/No-Go:** valid license · clear beneficial owner · verified stockpile · bankable terms · positive margin · enforceable contract · acceptable reputation risk

## mmi-margin-sensitivity
**Purpose:** pricing / margin / cash-cycle sensitivity for nickel trades. **When:** comparing deal economics, price formulas, shipment options, freight, specs, payment terms.
- **Inputs:** commodity · tonnage · Ni grade · moisture · price formula · base price · premium/discount · freight · barging/trucking · surveyor/lab · royalty/tax · working-capital cost · payment timing · FX
- **Base case:** revenue · purchase cost · freight · surveyor/lab · taxes/fees · financing · gross & net margin · margin per WMT/DMT · cash conversion cycle
- **Sensitivity:** Bear / Base / Bull across price, grade, freight, payment delay → net margin → decision
- **Break-even:** price · freight · grade · max acceptable payment delay
- **Recommendation:** Strong Pass / Small Option Bet / Core Position
