# 📖 Glossary — Indonesian biz / mining / jetsport / legal

> Terms that recur across my entities. Quick reference for me + any AI working the vault.

## Corporate / tax / ops (see [[usaha-id]])
- **PT** — Perseroan Terbatas (Indonesian limited liability company).
- **NIB** — Nomor Induk Berusaha (business ID/license). **Akta** — notarial deed. **MenkumHAM** — Ministry of Law & Human Rights (legalizes entities).
- **BPJS** — social security: **Kes** (health) + **TK** (employment). **PPh 21 TER** — employee income-tax (withholding, TER method). **PPN** — VAT. **PPh Badan** — corporate income tax (22%).
- **PSAK** — Indonesian accounting standards. **Coretax** — DJP's tax system. **SPT Masa/Tahunan** — monthly/annual tax returns. **konsultan pajak** — tax consultant; **notaris** — notary.

## Nickel trade (see [[MMI — Mangkuluhur Mineral Indonesia]] · [[mmi pack (full)]])
- **FOB / CIF** — buy free-on-board at mine jetty / sell cost-insurance-freight at smelter.
- **IUP-OP** — mining business license (operation-production). **RKAB** — annual work plan & budget. **KTT** — mine technical head. **SPB** — shipping/loading permit.
- **HPM / HMA** — government benchmark price / metal reference price (per **Kepmen ESDM 144**). **PNBP / royalti** — non-tax state revenue / royalty (per **PP 19/2025**).
- **NPI** (nickel pig iron), **MHP** (mixed hydroxide precipitate), **saprolite/limonite** (ore types). **COA** — certificate of analysis (quality); **Sucofindo** — surveyor. **MODI/MOMS** — ESDM mineral data systems.

## Jetsport / federation (see [[IJBA Master Plan 2026-2030]])
- **KONI** — Indonesian national sports committee. **IJSBA** — international jetsport body. **Munas** — national congress. **DPP** — central board; **Kepengurusan** — the governing board/term.
- **Nusantara Cup** — IJBA national series. **AJGP** — Asian Jetsport Grand Prix. **JetHub** — public-access center; **JetPass** — subscription. **Runabout / Ski (stand-up)** — PWC race classes; **homologation** — equipment certification.

## Law / electoral (see [[disertasi-threshold]])
- **MK** — Mahkamah Konstitusi (Constitutional Court); **Putusan MK 116/PUU-XXI/2023** — the ruling the dissertation reconstructs.
- **Ambang batas parlemen** — parliamentary threshold. **Dapil / district magnitude** — electoral district seat count. **Effective / Maximal / Dynamic threshold** — the proposed formulas. **Normative / doctrinal** legal research method.

## Public sector (see [[survey-kepuasan-daerah]])
- **IKM** — Indeks Kepuasan Masyarakat (community satisfaction index). **Permendagri / PermenPAN** — ministry regulations. **Kepala Daerah** — regional head (Governor/Bupati/Walikota). **Provinsi → Kota/Kabupaten → Kecamatan → Kelurahan/Desa** — admin hierarchy.

## Brand / personal
- **Merah-Putih** — Indonesian red-white (identity accent). **D2C** — direct-to-consumer.
