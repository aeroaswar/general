# Jetski Racing & Athletic Identity

_My core athletic identity and the narrative everything else is built on._

## Standards
- Story is **pure jetski** — water, velocity, palmarès, real race photography.
- Two brand modes: **"Born on Water"** (dark, cinematic) and **"Daylight Wake"**
  (light, sporty). Merah-Putih red wake as signature accent.
- Authentic > polished: real results and real images over stock.

## Themes
- Velocity / wake / ocean-shader as visual language.
- Palmarès (race results) as proof; gallery as parallax race photos.
- Identity anchors the personal site and bleeds into GLU's sports energy.
