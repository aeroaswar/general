## Brand identity
- **Aesthetic:** premium minimalism, quiet luxury, negative space, earned restraint.
- **Voice:** confident without proving it; calm, direct, noun-led; never cute/loud.
- **NOT:** fast fashion, micro-influencer-driven, cheap, "fun", price-led, novelty.

## Operating context
- Margin-sensitive Indonesia D2C; Shopify primary + IG/TikTok ancillary; offline pop-up/retail later.
- Customers: 25–40 urban, design-conscious, quality-led. Singapore expansion runbook exists.
- Premium-anchored pricing — **discounting is brand-toxic**; AOV via bundles, not BOGO.
- State: brand audit + homepage redesign + two-volume 2026–2030 playbook complete.

## Strategic posture
The asset is the brand (the minimalism), not the product. Most growth requests are additions;
the right answer is often **subtraction** — protect the quiet. Full context: [[glu (skill)]].
