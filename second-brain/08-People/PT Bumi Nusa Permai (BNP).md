# PT Bumi Nusa Permai (BNP)

**Type:** Organization (counterparty) · **Relationship:** consortium partner

## Context
MMI's **consortium partner** and the **majority shareholder of ANI's IUP-OP mine** — the
arrangement through which [[MMI — Mangkuluhur Mineral Indonesia]] gains the right to lift
and sell nickel ore from [[ANI — Adhita Nikel Indonesia]]. Consortium agreement carries a
confidentiality clause (Pasal 11 Kerahasiaan).
