### How to read the graph
The raw project folders are mostly **code/assets** (`.html` / `.js` / images) — they don't
form a knowledge graph. The connected core is this Map + the Second-Brain notes. In **Graph
view → Settings**, turn **Attachments off** and **Existing files only on** to see the clean
knowledge graph (this Map at the center, areas/projects/skills radiating out).
